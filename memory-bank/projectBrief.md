# Project Brief

## Purpose

Deliver a cross-platform help desk solution that lets customers and internal staff log, assign, track, and resolve support tickets with offline capability, push/email notifications, and a secure Express/PostgreSQL backend.

## Target Users

- End customers and employees who need to submit and monitor support tickets on mobile.
- Help desk agents who triage, update, and resolve assigned tickets.
- Admins who oversee workload, manage users/roles, and configure notifications/deployment.
